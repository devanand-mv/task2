Recipe: Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir well.
  4. Pour into a greased baking tray.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Let cool and serve.
